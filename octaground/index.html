Project Octaground is a story inspired by 2020 April Fools' Minecraft snapshot, linear algebra, and obscure horrors of existence. Find yourself in a facility drifting through that place we all came from, and meet the facility's founders who had parted their ways, now desperately trying to fight the thing they painfully well know they cannot defeat.
